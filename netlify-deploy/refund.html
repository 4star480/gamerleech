Refund Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

We want you satisfied with your purchase. Because our products are digital and delivered instantly, refunds are limited as described below.

24-hour satisfaction window

If you are not satisfied, contact us within 24 hours of purchase at gamerleech2@gmail.com or via Discord with your order ID and email used at checkout. We will review eligible requests case by case.

Eligible for refund

  • Product not delivered due to our error
  • Wrong product sent
  • Technical issue we cannot resolve within 24 hours of reported support ticket
  • Duplicate charge or clear payment processing error

Not eligible for refund

  • Request made after 24 hours from purchase
  • Key or download already delivered and working as described
  • Game ban, detection, or patch after delivery (see Terms — inherent risk)
  • Incorrect crypto payment (wrong amount, wrong network, wrong address)
  • Chargebacks or payment disputes after receiving the product
  • Change of mind after activation or use

How to request

  1. Open a ticket on Discord or email us with order ID, payment proof, and reason.
  2. Allow up to 48 hours for review (often faster).
  3. Approved refunds are returned via the original payment method where possible, or store credit by agreement.

Subscriptions & renewals

Weekly and monthly licenses expire automatically. We do not auto-renew unless explicitly stated on the product page. No partial refunds for unused time unless required by law.
